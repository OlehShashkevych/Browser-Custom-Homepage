Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

HomeSpace is a personal start page dashboard. This policy explains how your data is handled. My philosophy is simple: your data belongs to you.

2. Data Collection & Storage

  • Account Data: We store your username and a securely hashed version of your password in our database. We never store passwords in plain text.
  • Workspace State: Your links, groups, and dashboard settings are stored in our database so you can access them across devices.
  • Local Storage: We use browser Local Storage and Cache to enable PWA features and offline access. This stays on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

HomeSpace interacts with a few external APIs to provide widgets:

  • Weather: Provided by Open-Meteo (no personal data sent).
  • F1 Data: Provided by Ergast/Jolpi API.
  • Icons: Icons are fetched via Icon.horse or directly from the respective websites.

4. Security

We use HTTPS for all communications. While we implement industry-standard protections (SQL injection prevention, XSS filtering), no system is 100% secure. We encourage using a unique password for this service.

5. Contact

If you have questions about your data, feel free to contact me via shashkevych.com.
